# Global Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales and profitability data from the Global Superstore dataset using Microsoft Excel. The analysis focuses on identifying key business trends, evaluating profitability, and developing actionable recommendations based on sales performance.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales and profit performance across different markets, regions, categories, and subcategories while identifying opportunities to improve business performance.

## Key Performance Indicators

- Total Sales: $12,642,502
- Total Profit: $1,467,457
- Total Orders: 25,035
- Average Order Value: $505
- Profit Margin: 11.61%

## Key Business Insights

- The Tables subcategory generated approximately $64,083 in negative profit and was the only subcategory operating at a loss.
- Sales and profitability performance varied across markets and regions.
- Returned orders were analyzed by market to identify potential areas for further investigation.
- Profitability analysis provided insight into which product categories and subcategories contributed most effectively to overall business performance.

## Business Recommendations

- Investigate the factors contributing to losses within the Tables subcategory, including pricing, discounting, shipping costs, and individual product performance.
- Evaluate profitability across markets and regions to identify opportunities for growth and improved performance.
- Monitor returned orders by market to identify potential product or customer service issues.
- Focus on high-performing product categories and markets while developing strategies to improve underperforming areas.

## Tools & Methodology

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning and Preparation
- Pivot Tables
- KPI Analysis
- Data Visualization
- Profitability Analysis
- Business Insight Development

The dataset was cleaned and prepared before analysis. Pivot tables were created to evaluate sales, profit, orders, profitability, markets, regions, categories, and subcategories. An interactive dashboard was then developed to summarize key findings and business insights.
